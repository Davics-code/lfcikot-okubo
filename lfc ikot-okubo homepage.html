From Resignation to Promotion!

In October 2017, I was placed on probation because I met thirty two percent of my target. On January 19, 2018, I was told to resign because I only met forty eight percent. I came to church and reminded God of the words of His servant. Bishop David Oyedepo said that I shall serve and be rewarded not be told to resign. I became committed to Covenant Hour of Prayer. On my way to the office every morning I shared fliers, I invited people to church and saw to their establishment. The following week, after the Covenant Hour of Prayer, one of the billionaires in Festac province met with me. He asked how he could open an account in my bank and I told him. Immediately, he asked for the account opening form, I gave it to him. Right there, he filled and signed it. Thereafter, I went to his house and he gave me all the necessary documents. I went to the office and opened the account. That same day, he transferred an amount that changed my position. The following week, the Head of Human Resources said I should be reappraised. I was reappraised and I scored one hundred and fifty percent of my target. On May 17, 2019, I was promoted. I give God all the glory! --Francis Obiasor READ MORE TESTIMONIES!
